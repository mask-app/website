Mask Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-18 | Applies to: Mask website and app

This Privacy Policy explains how Mask ("the App") handles data when you use our products. By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

1. Information We Process

Mask is designed as a local-first app. By default, the following data is stored locally on your own device:

  • API keys you manually configure (stored via local secure storage)
  • Conversation history, translation history, and settings
  • Runtime logs and diagnostics used for local troubleshooting

2. Data We Do Not Retain

Mask servers do not retain your API keys, conversation history, raw screenshots, or translation text. Unless you explicitly send requests to third-party services, such data is not uploaded for persistent storage on Mask servers.

3. Third-Party Model Providers and Liability Boundary

When you use model services such as OpenAI, Google Gemini, DeepSeek, Xiaomi Mimo, or Ollama (remote deployment), relevant requests are sent to the provider you selected or configured.

  • Each provider is solely responsible for its own data handling and compliance obligations.
  • Privacy policies for those model services are defined and maintained by the respective providers.
  • Mask cannot control how third-party providers store, analyze, or further process submitted content.

Important: You should review and accept each provider's privacy policy and terms before use. Mask is not responsible for the data processing practices of third-party model providers.

4. Purposes of Processing

We process data only as needed for product functionality and reliability, including:

  • Running OCR, translation, and context-related processing
  • Saving your local preferences and session state
  • Diagnosing client-side issues and improving user experience

5. Storage and Retention

  • Local data remains under your control and can be deleted at any time.
  • If you uninstall the app or clear local storage, related data is removed from your device (subject to your system behavior).
  • Mask does not maintain centralized cloud archives of user conversations or translations.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards, including secure local storage patterns, minimized upload strategy, and access controls. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices

  • You may review, modify, or delete locally stored settings and history.
  • You may rotate, revoke, or disable third-party API keys at any time.
  • You may stop using the App and remove local data from your device.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction). If you are a parent or guardian and believe data was processed inappropriately, contact us.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect product or compliance changes. Updated versions will be posted on the website or in-app, and material changes will receive prominent notice.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: hello@mask-app.com
